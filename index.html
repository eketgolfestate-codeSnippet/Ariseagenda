Eket Golf Estate in Partnership with AKSG.

We respectfully propose a strategic partnership with the Government of Akwa Ibom State to deliver a premium residential development comprising 100 units of luxury 3-bedroom apartments, each with an internal floor area of 220.252 m², within the serene and prestigious residential precinct of Eket Golf Estate. This development will be executed under the distinctive and forward-looking brand identity, “ARISE CARE.”

This initiative is designed to redefine modern living standards within the region by combining architectural excellence, functional design, and sustainable building practices. Each apartment will be thoughtfully crafted to provide spacious interiors, high-quality finishes, and a comfortable living environment that meets the expectations of contemporary homeowners and investors alike.

The “ARISE CARE” project aims to create not just housing, but a vibrant and secure community that promotes wellbeing, convenience, and lifestyle enhancement. The development will incorporate essential infrastructure, green spaces, reliable utilities, and modern amenities to ensure a seamless living experience for residents.

In addition to addressing the growing demand for high-quality housing, this partnership will contribute significantly to the economic development of Eket Local Government Area and Akwa Ibom State by creating jobs, attracting investment, and enhancing the overall value of the State. It will also reinforce the government's commitment to urban development, innovation, and improved quality of life for its citizens.

We are confident that this collaboration will serve as a landmark project, setting a new benchmark for residential developments in Akwa Ibom State while aligning with the broader vision of sustainable growth and prosperity.

04
Proposed Partnership Models

We are flexible and open to any model that best serves the State’s objectives:

  • Option A – Government-Backed Development Guarantee:
The Government of Akwa Ibom State shall formally mandate Eket Golf Estate through a Letter of Guarantee and a binding contract executed between both parties. Under this arrangement, Eket Golf Estate will undertake the construction of the project, and upon reaching 70% completion of development, the government shall release the agreed funds to the developer.
  • Option B – Phased Government Funding Structure:
The Government of Akwa Ibom State shall adopt a milestone-based payment approach, beginning with an initial 30% upfront disbursement. At a defined stage of project development, an additional 40% payment shall be released, while the final 30% balance shall be paid upon full completion of the project.

As a key provision, upon full completion of the project, all keys to the 100 housing units shall be formally handed over to the Government of Akwa Ibom State.
